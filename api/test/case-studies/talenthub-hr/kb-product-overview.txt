# TalentHub - HR & Recruiting Platform

TalentHub is an all-in-one HR and recruiting platform that helps companies attract, hire, and retain top talent. Our AI-powered tools streamline every step of the hiring process.

## Key Features

### 1. Applicant Tracking System (ATS)
- Centralized candidate pipeline management
- Customizable hiring stages (Sourcing → Screening → Interview → Offer → Hired)
- Automated status updates and notifications
- Resume parsing with AI extraction
- Duplicate candidate detection
- Bulk actions for efficient processing

### 2. AI-Powered Candidate Matching
- Smart matching algorithm scores candidates against job requirements
- Skills-based matching with 95% accuracy
- Experience level and culture fit assessment
- Automatic ranking of top candidates
- Bias-free screening with anonymized reviews

### 3. Job Posting & Distribution
- One-click posting to 50+ job boards (Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, etc.)
- Custom career page builder
- Social media job sharing
- Employee referral tracking
- SEO-optimized job descriptions

### 4. Interview Management
- Integrated calendar scheduling with Google and Outlook
- Self-service interview booking for candidates
- Video interview platform with recording
- Interview scorecards and structured feedback
- Automated interview reminders

### 5. Onboarding Automation
- Digital offer letters with e-signatures
- New hire document collection
- Onboarding task checklists
- IT equipment and access provisioning
- First-day welcome workflows

### 6. Analytics & Reporting
- Time-to-hire metrics
- Source effectiveness analysis
- Pipeline conversion rates
- Diversity and inclusion reporting
- Custom report builder

## Supported Integrations

- **HRIS**: Workday, BambooHR, ADP, Gusto
- **Communication**: Slack, Microsoft Teams
- **Calendar**: Google Calendar, Outlook
- **Background Checks**: Checkr, GoodHire
- **Assessments**: Codility, HackerRank, TestGorilla
- **Video**: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams
