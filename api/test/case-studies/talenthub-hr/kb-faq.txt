# TalentHub - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

**Q: What is TalentHub?**
A: TalentHub is an all-in-one HR and recruiting platform that helps companies attract, hire, and retain talent. It includes an applicant tracking system (ATS), AI candidate matching, video interviews, and onboarding automation.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes! All plans include a 14-day free trial with full access to features. No credit card required to start.

**Q: How is TalentHub different from other ATS platforms?**
A: TalentHub combines ATS functionality with AI-powered candidate matching, built-in video interviews, and onboarding automation in one platform. Most competitors require multiple tools. We're also more affordable and easier to use.

**Q: Can I import candidates from my current ATS?**
A: Yes, we support data import from most major ATS platforms including Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, and BambooHR. Our team will help you migrate for free.

## Pricing & Billing

**Q: How much does TalentHub cost?**
A: Plans start at $99/month for small teams. The Growth plan at $299/month is our most popular. Enterprise pricing is custom. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

**Q: Can I change plans anytime?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades are effective immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Yes! Startups get 30% off the first year, nonprofits get 40% off, and annual plans save 20%.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), ACH transfers, and wire transfers for Enterprise customers.

## Features & Capabilities

**Q: How many job boards can I post to?**
A: Depends on your plan: Starter posts to 10 free boards, Growth to 30+ boards, and Professional/Enterprise to 50+ boards including premium boards.

**Q: Does TalentHub have video interviewing?**
A: Yes, built-in video interviews are included in Growth plans and above. Features include live interviews, one-way video screening, automatic recording, and transcription.

**Q: Can I customize the hiring pipeline?**
A: Yes, you can create custom pipeline stages, automated actions, and email templates. Starter plan has basic customization, while Growth and above have full flexibility.

**Q: Does TalentHub integrate with my HRIS?**
A: Yes, we integrate with Workday, BambooHR, ADP, Gusto, and other popular HRIS platforms. API access is available on Professional and Enterprise plans.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Yes, TalentHub has iOS and Android apps for reviewing candidates, scheduling interviews, and managing your pipeline on the go.

## AI & Matching

**Q: How does AI candidate matching work?**
A: Our AI analyzes job requirements and candidate profiles to calculate a match score (0-100%). It considers skills, experience, education, and other factors. The algorithm learns from your hiring decisions to improve over time.

**Q: Is AI matching unbiased?**
A: Yes, our matching algorithm is designed to be bias-free. It ignores names, photos, ages, and other demographic information. We regularly audit the algorithm for fairness and publish our findings.

**Q: Can I turn off AI features?**
A: Yes, AI features are optional. You can disable matching scores and recommendations in Settings > AI Preferences.

## Security & Compliance

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, we use bank-level security including AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, and regular security audits. We're SOC 2 Type II certified.

**Q: Is TalentHub GDPR compliant?**
A: Yes, we're fully GDPR compliant. Candidates can request their data, and you can configure automatic data retention policies.

**Q: Do you support SSO?**
A: Yes, SAML-based SSO is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. We integrate with Okta, Azure AD, OneLogin, and Google Workspace.

**Q: Can candidates delete their data?**
A: Yes, candidates can request data deletion through the candidate portal or by emailing privacy@talenthub.io. Deletion is processed within 30 days.

## Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest 2 versions). We recommend Chrome for the best experience.

**Q: Can I use TalentHub offline?**
A: The mobile app has limited offline functionality for viewing candidates. Full features require internet connectivity.

**Q: Is there an API?**
A: Yes, our REST API is available on Professional and Enterprise plans. Documentation is at docs.talenthub.io/api.

**Q: How often is data backed up?**
A: Data is backed up every hour to multiple geographic locations. We maintain 30 days of backup history.
