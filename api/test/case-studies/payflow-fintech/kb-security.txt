# PayFlow - Security & Privacy

Your security is our top priority. Here's how we protect your money and data.

## Account Security

### Authentication
- **Biometric Login**: Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint
- **Strong Passwords**: Minimum 12 characters required
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: SMS, authenticator app, or hardware key
- **Session Management**: Auto-logout after 15 minutes of inactivity
- **Device Recognition**: Alerts for new device logins

### Card Security
- **Instant Lock/Unlock**: Freeze your card instantly from the app
- **Virtual Card Numbers**: Generate temporary card numbers for online shopping
- **Transaction Limits**: Set daily spending and ATM limits
- **Location-Based Controls**: Only allow transactions in specific countries
- **Merchant Category Blocks**: Block gambling, adult content, etc.

### Fraud Protection
- **Real-Time Monitoring**: AI monitors transactions 24/7
- **Instant Alerts**: Push notification for every transaction
- **Zero Liability**: You're never responsible for unauthorized charges
- **Dispute Resolution**: File disputes directly in app
- **Purchase Protection**: Coverage for damaged or stolen items (Premium)

## Data Protection

### Encryption
- **In Transit**: TLS 1.3 encryption for all communications
- **At Rest**: AES-256 encryption for stored data
- **End-to-End**: Messages and documents encrypted client-to-client
- **Key Management**: Hardware security modules (HSM) protect encryption keys

### Infrastructure
- **Cloud Security**: Hosted on bank-grade infrastructure
- **Data Centers**: Geographically distributed with redundancy
- **Access Control**: Multi-layer authentication for employees
- **Audit Logs**: All access to customer data is logged

## Regulatory Compliance

### Banking Regulations
- **FDIC Insured**: Deposits insured up to $250,000 per account
- **Bank Partners**: Funds held at partner banks (FDIC members)
- **State Licenses**: Licensed money transmitter in all 50 states
- **Federal Oversight**: Registered with FinCEN

### Privacy Compliance
- **GLBA**: Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act compliance
- **CCPA**: California Consumer Privacy Act
- **SOC 2 Type II**: Annual security audits
- **PCI DSS**: Level 1 compliance for payment card data

## Privacy Practices

### Data We Collect
- Account information (name, email, address, SSN)
- Transaction history
- Device information for security
- Usage analytics (anonymized)

### Data We DON'T Sell
- We never sell your personal information
- We never sell your transaction data
- We never share data for marketing by third parties

### Data Sharing
We only share data with:
- Banking partners to provide services
- Payment networks to process transactions
- Law enforcement when legally required
- With your consent for connected services

### Data Control
- Download your data anytime
- Delete your account and data upon request
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Control what's shared with connected apps

## Security Best Practices

### Recommended Actions
1. Enable two-factor authentication
2. Use biometric login
3. Enable transaction alerts
4. Review connected apps regularly
5. Use unique, strong passwords
6. Keep app updated

### If Your Account Is Compromised
1. Lock your card immediately in the app
2. Change your password
3. Call PayFlow Security: 1-888-PAY-FLOW
4. Review recent transactions
5. File disputes for unauthorized charges
