# PayFlow - Digital Banking & Payments App

PayFlow is a modern digital banking platform that helps individuals and businesses manage money, make payments, and grow their wealth - all from one app.

## Key Features

### 1. Digital Bank Account
- FDIC-insured accounts up to $250,000
- No minimum balance requirements
- No monthly fees
- Instant account opening (2 minutes)
- Virtual and physical debit cards
- Direct deposit with 2-day early access

### 2. Money Transfers
- Instant transfers between PayFlow users (free)
- Bank transfers (ACH) in 1-3 business days (free)
- Wire transfers (domestic and international)
- Send money to 100+ countries
- Request money from anyone via email/SMS
- Split bills with friends

### 3. Bill Pay
- Pay any bill from your PayFlow account
- Schedule recurring payments
- Automatic payment reminders
- Pay with bank account, debit card, or credit card
- E-bills: receive bills directly in app

### 4. Savings & Goals
- High-yield savings account (4.5% APY)
- Create savings goals with target dates
- Round-up spare change to savings
- Automatic savings transfers
- No minimum balance or fees

### 5. Investing
- Stock and ETF trading (commission-free)
- Fractional shares starting at $1
- Automated investing portfolios
- Cryptocurrency trading (Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.)
- Retirement accounts (IRA, Roth IRA)

### 6. Credit Products
- PayFlow Credit Card (1.5% cashback)
- Personal loans ($1,000 - $50,000)
- Credit builder card for new credit
- Credit score monitoring (free)
- No foreign transaction fees

### 7. Business Features
- Business checking account
- Invoice creation and tracking
- Accept payments (card, ACH, invoices)
- Employee expense cards
- Accounting integrations (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Tax reporting tools

## Security Features

- 256-bit AES encryption
- Biometric login (Face ID, fingerprint)
- Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Real-time fraud monitoring
- Card lock/unlock from app
- Transaction alerts
- FDIC insured deposits

## Supported Platforms

- iOS 14.0 or later
- Android 8.0 or later
- Web app: app.payflow.com
- Apple Watch companion app

## Getting Started

1. Download PayFlow from App Store or Google Play
2. Create account with email and phone
3. Verify identity (photo ID + selfie, takes 2 minutes)
4. Link external bank account or set up direct deposit
5. Start managing your money
