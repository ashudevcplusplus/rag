# PayFlow - Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

**Q: What is PayFlow?**
A: PayFlow is a digital banking platform that offers checking, savings, payments, investing, and credit products - all from one app. It's designed to be fee-free and mobile-first.

**Q: Is PayFlow a real bank?**
A: PayFlow partners with FDIC-insured banks to hold your deposits. Your money is protected up to $250,000 per depositor, just like traditional banks.

**Q: How do I open an account?**
A: Download the app, create an account with your email and phone, verify your identity with a photo ID and selfie, and you're done. It takes about 2 minutes.

**Q: Is there an age requirement?**
A: You must be 18 or older to open a personal account. Teen accounts (13-17) are available with parental consent.

**Q: Can I use PayFlow if I have bad credit?**
A: Yes! PayFlow doesn't check your credit to open a checking or savings account. Credit products may have eligibility requirements.

## Money & Transfers

**Q: How do I add money to my account?**
A: Direct deposit, bank transfer (ACH), mobile check deposit, cash deposit at retail partners (CVS, Walgreens, 7-Eleven), or wire transfer.

**Q: How long do transfers take?**
A: PayFlow-to-PayFlow: Instant. ACH transfers: 1-3 business days. Wire transfers: Same day. Direct deposit: 2 days early!

**Q: Is there a limit on transfers?**
A: ACH transfers: $25,000/day. Wire transfers: $50,000/day. PayFlow transfers: $5,000/day. Limits can be increased upon request.

**Q: Can I send money internationally?**
A: Yes, send money to 100+ countries. Fees and exchange rates vary by destination. International transfers take 1-5 business days.

**Q: What is the savings APY?**
A: Our high-yield savings account offers 4.5% APY with no minimum balance. Rates are variable and may change.

## Debit Card

**Q: How do I get a debit card?**
A: A free virtual debit card is available instantly upon account opening. Physical card arrives in 7-10 business days.

**Q: Where can I use my PayFlow card?**
A: Anywhere Visa is accepted worldwide - stores, online, ATMs. Also works with Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay.

**Q: Are there ATM fees?**
A: No fees at 55,000+ in-network ATMs (Allpoint, MoneyPass). Out-of-network ATMs charge $2.50.

**Q: What if my card is lost or stolen?**
A: Lock your card instantly in the app. Request a replacement card (free, or $15 for expedited 2-day delivery).

## Security

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Yes! Deposits are FDIC insured up to $250,000. We use bank-grade security including 256-bit encryption and real-time fraud monitoring.

**Q: What is two-factor authentication?**
A: An extra layer of security requiring a code from your phone or authenticator app when logging in. We strongly recommend enabling it.

**Q: What if there's unauthorized activity?**
A: Lock your card, report it in the app, and we'll investigate. You have zero liability for unauthorized transactions.

**Q: How do I dispute a transaction?**
A: Go to the transaction in your activity, tap "Report a problem," and follow the prompts. Most disputes are resolved within 10 business days.

## Investing

**Q: Can I buy stocks on PayFlow?**
A: Yes! Trade stocks and ETFs commission-free. Fractional shares start at $1. Cryptocurrency trading also available.

**Q: Is there a minimum to invest?**
A: $1 for fractional shares. Some cryptocurrencies have minimums based on network fees.

**Q: What about retirement accounts?**
A: We offer Traditional IRA and Roth IRA with no fees. Automated investing portfolios available.

**Q: Are there trading fees?**
A: Stocks and ETFs: $0. Crypto: 0.5%-1.5% spread. Automated investing: 0.25% annual fee.

## Business Accounts

**Q: Can I use PayFlow for my business?**
A: Yes! PayFlow Business includes checking, invoicing, payment acceptance, expense cards, and accounting integrations.

**Q: How much does PayFlow Business cost?**
A: $15/month for PayFlow Business, $35/month for Business Pro. First 3 months free for new businesses.

**Q: Can I accept credit card payments?**
A: Yes, accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Discover. Rates start at 2.6% + $0.30 per transaction.

**Q: Does PayFlow integrate with accounting software?**
A: Yes, we integrate with QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and other popular accounting tools.
