# PayFlow - Pricing & Fees

PayFlow is designed to be fee-free for most common banking activities.

## Account Types

### PayFlow Personal (Free)
Best for individuals
- No monthly fees
- No minimum balance
- Free debit card
- Free ATM withdrawals (55,000+ network)
- Free instant transfers to PayFlow users
- Free ACH transfers
- High-yield savings (4.5% APY)
- Credit score monitoring

### PayFlow Premium - $9.99/month
Best for power users
- Everything in Personal, plus:
- Premium metal debit card
- 1% cashback on all purchases
- Free wire transfers (domestic)
- Priority customer support
- Extended overdraft grace period (72 hours)
- Free international ATM withdrawals
- Travel insurance
- Cell phone protection

### PayFlow Business - $15/month
Best for small businesses
- Business checking account
- Unlimited employee cards
- Invoice creation
- Accept card payments (2.6% + $0.30)
- Accept ACH payments (1%)
- Accounting integrations
- Tax document generation
- Dedicated business support

### PayFlow Business Pro - $35/month
Best for growing businesses
- Everything in Business, plus:
- Lower payment fees (2.4% + $0.25)
- ACH payments (0.8%)
- Custom payment links
- API access
- Multi-user permissions
- Advanced analytics
- Dedicated account manager
