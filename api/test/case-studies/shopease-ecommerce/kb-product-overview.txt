# ShopEase - E-commerce Platform Overview

ShopEase is a modern e-commerce platform that helps businesses sell products online with powerful tools for inventory management, payments, and customer engagement.

## Key Features

### 1. Online Storefront
- Drag-and-drop store builder with 100+ themes
- Mobile-responsive designs
- Custom domain support
- SSL certificate included
- Multi-language support (50+ languages)
- Multi-currency checkout

### 2. Product Management
- Unlimited product listings
- Product variants (size, color, material)
- Bulk import/export via CSV
- Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
- Digital product delivery
- Product reviews and ratings

### 3. Order Management
- Centralized order dashboard
- Order status tracking
- Automated order confirmations
- Shipping label generation
- Returns and refunds processing
- Order notes and tags

### 4. Payment Processing
- Accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Buy Now, Pay Later (Klarna, Afterpay)
- Cryptocurrency payments (Bitcoin, Ethereum)
- Multiple currency support
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliant

### 5. Shipping & Fulfillment
- Real-time shipping rates (UPS, FedEx, USPS, DHL)
- Print shipping labels in-app
- Order tracking for customers
- Fulfillment by ShopEase warehouse option
- International shipping support
- Local pickup and delivery options

### 6. Marketing Tools
- Email marketing campaigns
- Abandoned cart recovery
- Discount codes and coupons
- Gift cards
- Customer loyalty program
- Social media integration

### 7. Analytics
- Sales reports and dashboards
- Customer behavior analytics
- Product performance metrics
- Traffic sources analysis
- Conversion rate tracking
- Export to Excel/CSV

## Integrations

- **Marketing**: Mailchimp, Klaviyo, HubSpot
- **Accounting**: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks
- **Inventory**: TradeGecko, Cin7
- **Shipping**: ShipStation, Easyship
- **Customer Support**: Zendesk, Freshdesk
- **Social**: Facebook Shop, Instagram Shopping, TikTok Shop

## System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- Mobile apps available for iOS 14+ and Android 8+
