# ShopEase - Pricing Plans

Choose the right plan for your online business. All plans include a 14-day free trial.

## Plan Comparison

### Basic - $29/month
Best for new businesses just getting started
- Up to 100 products
- 2 staff accounts
- 2% transaction fee (+ payment processor fees)
- Basic storefront themes (10 options)
- Email support
- Manual order creation
- Discount codes
- SSL certificate
- Abandoned cart recovery

### Professional - $79/month (Most Popular)
Best for growing businesses
- Up to 5,000 products
- 5 staff accounts
- 1% transaction fee
- All premium themes (100+ options)
- Priority email support
- Gift cards
- Professional reports
- Customer segments
- Shipping discounts (up to 50% off)
- Third-party calculated shipping rates
- International domains

### Business - $299/month
Best for high-volume businesses
- Unlimited products
- 15 staff accounts
- 0.5% transaction fee
- Advanced report builder
- Phone support
- Advanced customer segmentation
- Automation workflows
- Custom checkout
- Third-party insurance rates
- Fulfillment by ShopEase access
- Dedicated success manager

### Enterprise - Custom Pricing
Best for large enterprises
- Everything in Business, plus:
- Unlimited staff accounts
- 0% transaction fee (payment processor only)
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantee (99.99% uptime)
- Dedicated account manager
- White-label options
- API priority access
- Multi-store management
- Custom reporting

## Transaction Fees

In addition to monthly subscription, transaction fees apply:

| Plan | ShopEase Fee | Payment Processor |
|------|--------------|-------------------|
| Basic | 2.0% | 2.9% + $0.30 |
| Professional | 1.0% | 2.6% + $0.30 |
| Business | 0.5% | 2.4% + $0.30 |
| Enterprise | 0% | 2.2% + $0.30 |

Using ShopEase Payments eliminates the ShopEase transaction fee.
